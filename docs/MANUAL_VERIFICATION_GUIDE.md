# Manual Program Website Verification Guide

Use this checklist to validate each of the 62 programs against its official school website before scoring or publishing a final ranking.

## Verification record

For every program, record:

- Institution and exact official program name.
- Official program URL and verification date.
- Page title or section supporting each collected value.
- Whether the evidence is current, archived, ambiguous, or unavailable.
- A short note when the official website conflicts with an existing workbook value.

## Curriculum relevance

- Required courses and total required credits.
- Sustainability, climate, environment, ESG, policy, or systems content.
- Quantitative, data, economics, finance, management, or policy methods.
- Elective flexibility and specialization options.
- Capstone, thesis, practicum, studio, or consulting requirement.

## Cost and accessibility

- Tuition amount, currency, and academic year.
- Whether tuition is per credit, term, year, or full program.
- Mandatory fees that are separately published.
- Program duration and full-time/part-time options.
- Scholarships, assistantships, or funding specifically available to program students.

Do not combine tuition and living costs unless the source clearly separates them. Record currency and source year without converting values in the raw-data columns.

## Experiential learning

- Internship, co-op, field course, consulting project, or community partnership.
- Whether the experience is required or optional.
- Named partner organizations or project examples.
- Study-abroad or multi-campus options.

## Career evidence

- Published employment report or graduate destination data.
- Reporting year, response rate, and population covered.
- Employer examples and role categories.
- Career services specifically available to the program.

Do not infer program employment outcomes from university-wide statistics.

## Institutional signals

- Confirm AASHE STARS rating, score, and validity date on the official AASHE profile.
- Confirm UNPRME signatory status and most recent SIP report.
- Check whether OpenAlex matched the correct institution/ROR identifier.
- Keep QS/THE institution rankings separate from subject-ranking proxies.

## Quality-control status

Use a consistent status field:

- `Verified`: Current official source directly supports the value.
- `Needs follow-up`: Source exists but the value or scope is ambiguous.
- `Not published`: No official value was found after checking relevant pages.
- `Outdated`: Only an older official value was available.
- `Conflict`: Two official pages report different values.

Never replace `Not published` with an estimate. Preserve the source URL and verification date so another reviewer can reproduce the decision.
